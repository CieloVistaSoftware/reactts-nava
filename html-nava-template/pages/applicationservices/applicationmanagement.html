Application Management Services

Application Management Services

iDriven Global Tech provides top-notch application management services that include monitoring the application, managing the performance, support system, evolution of the app and optimization, and more. Our team offers comprehensive services to various applications of different sizes, complexity, and availability.

Application monitoring and performance management

Our professionals help you with maintaining the right performance level for your applications that are critical to your business. We perform regular monitoring activities on the application to find any issues and fix the slowdowns in the application before they affect the process flow or the user experience.

Application troubleshooting

Our team promptly resolves the issues in the application that are related to misbehaviors, slow processing of the requests, slow request handling time, runtime errors, poor data quantity, and missing components.

Application enhancements

We help you with updating the app with minor changes and also assist you completely with the release of the updated application versions.

Application optimization

Whenever the app is changed or modified, it is important to check if the application is still in line with the business’s needs and goals. Our experts run a quick audit and detailed review of the application and the business process. We also try running automation and integrations for easing the process and to check the business flows.

Change management and training services

iDriven Global Tech provides a flawless plan to execute the change management of the application along with expert review and training services. We work closely with your IT team to understand the flow and the process. Our team provides a step-by-step guide and conducts workshops, and onsite training to ensure a user experience quality with every released version.

Application Management Backed By Help Desk Services

We also provide 24/7 support for our management services to ensure the smooth functioning of the application. Our helpdesk agents are multilingual with updated knowledge of the IT infrastructure of your business. The support team of iDriven Global tech acts as your one-point contact for all your questions and queries on the application and its functions.

How our support system works:

  • Prioritizing and categorizing incidents and use cases that are reported through e-mail, phone, chat, or any ticket management system.
  • Providing an immediate solution for recurring issues upon instruction and escalating further complex cases to technical experts.
  • Keeping the users posted on the request status.
  • Expanding a knowledge base and FAQs for better self-help.
  • Gathering feedback from the users for further development of the app.
