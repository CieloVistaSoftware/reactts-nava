Application Maintenance and Support Service

Application support and maintenance services aimed at keeping your application updated and readily available and reliable for future evolution. iDriven Global Tech runs regular maintenance on your application to ensure 100% readiness.

Apps We Support and Maintain

Web apps – Both internal and external purpose web apps.

Cloud apps & SaaS – Apps that are deployed on Cloud,

Database apps – MySQL, and other database applications

Mobile apps – exclusive mobile applications

AR/VR apps – Apps that have AR/VR features implemented

Desktop apps – Apps that are made exclusively for system download.

Application support services

The support system of iDriven Global Tech is divided into 4 levels based on the severity of the issue.

L0

  • Preparation of the basic user guide for initial troubleshooting.
  • Complete product and technical information
  • Manuals, guides, and FAQs.
  • Knowledge base with search.
  • Continuous maintenance of all guiding information materials

L1

  • Tickets raising and registering messages through all communication channels.
  • Prioritization of requests and resolving user cases.
  • Efficient ITSM and SOPsprocesses.
  • Providing issue status updates within the agreed SLA till resolution.

L2

  • Administering application usage and user accounts.
  • Log investigation for issue root cause analysis.
  • Optimizing software installation.
  • Resolving incidents within 8 hours or the mentioned SLA.

L3

  • Resolving the root cause of the issue.
  • Identifying and fixing complex issues by simple tweaks to the code and database.
  • Prompt correction of customization issues.
  • Roll out the most comprehensive patches and hotfixes after 24h.
