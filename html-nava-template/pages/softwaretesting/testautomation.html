Test Automation

Test automation uses software to execute repeatable tests on a system, application, or product to ensure that it meets the desired quality standards.

Test automation aims to increase efficiency and reduce manual testing efforts while ensuring that the software remains functional and free of defects. It is achieved by automating test cases and validating the software's functionality, performance, and reliability.

Test automation also helps reduce the time required to complete testing and helps in detecting bugs at an early stage of development.
