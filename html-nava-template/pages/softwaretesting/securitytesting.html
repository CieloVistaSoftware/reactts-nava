Security testing

Security testing services help businesses to identify, analyze and fix the flaws in the software, and any IT-related processes. The basic testing of software involves checking for any vulnerabilities and fixing the same to prevent any hacks in the future.

How iDriven Global Tech helps/approach:

iDriven Global Tech (P) Ltd offers a wide range of security testing services from vulnerability checks, and penetration tests to compliance reviews and IT audit. Here are the things we check for while performing security testing:

• Network services

• Servers

• Firewalls, IDS/IPSs, and other security solutions

• Application protocol interfaces (APIs)

• Front end and back end of applications

Security Testing Components:

iDriven Global Tech (P) Ltd checks for the below security testing components to ensure delivering a maximum secured application.

• Vulnerability assessment: Our professional testers perform both automated and manual scanning on the client’s environment like IT infrastructure, web applications, mobile applications, and desktop applications.

• Security code review: The next phase is to check and analyze the source code for any flaws. Fixing any such flaws can prevent unauthorized access to the data and functionality of the app in the long run.

• Penetration testing: Our ethical hackers stimulate real-time scenarios to investigate the impact of those cybersecurity attacks. This test helps them to understand the potential harm any attack could cause and to plan an effective method to void such attacks.

• Social engineering testing: Our team implements the techniques used by most cybercriminals to verify whether your employees are due diligence to your company policies.

• DDoS testing: Our professionals try overloading your servers or the capacity of your application by sending malicious requests from different locations to test the resilience of DDoS attacks.

• Compliance testing: Our experts check your security controls for regulatory standards. It includes HIPAA, PCI DSS/SSF, GLBA, and GDPR.

• IT security audit: We perform comprehensive reviews on your IT networks and apps to check and improve the security levels through regular audits.

• Cloud security assessment: Since most of the software is deployed on Cloud, one of the most important things is to keep the cloud security settings in your control. Our experts help you with defining the AWS, Azure, and GCP controls to make it a secure environment for your apps and network servers.

Why iDriven Global Tech:

• To stay aware of the new vulnerabilities that may arise due to changes in the code or compliance policy of your IT.

• To stay compliant with the major security regulators.

• To avoid and prevent any devastating cyber-attacks.

• To build your reputation as a secure and transparent business by increasing trust and customer loyalty.

Our Service Models:

One-time security testing:

We run one-time security tests on your network and applications and fix the prevailing vulnerabilities.

Managed security testing:

Regular testing and reporting of vulnerabilities make your system even more secure. The managed security testing services are both cost-effective and time-effective when compared to the one-time services.
