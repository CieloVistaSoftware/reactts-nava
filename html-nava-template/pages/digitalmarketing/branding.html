Branding

For any business, be it a startup, small business, or large company, branding plays a very important role in digital growth. iDriven Global Tech (P) Ltd provides top-notch branding services for all types of businesses around the globe. The main purpose of branding is to take your product or service directly to your target audience with a direct and personal connection.

Branding services include:

  • Business, product, or service name selection
  • Logo design
  • Developing brand identity usage guidelines
  • Customer persona development
  • Brand messaging statements
  • Vision and mission statements
  • Brand marketing and promotion strategy
  • Website design and development
  • Brochure and stationery design
  • Developing marketing collaterals
  • Presentation design
  • Packaging and product design
  • Employee communication and messaging
  • Email and landing page design

Our Approach:

Strategy Planning:

The very basic step is to understand your business and run quick market research. Based on the analysis and research study, our professional plan a proper strategy for your business. We ensure finding the right way to make your brand more appealing and personalized for your target audience.

Brand Identity:

Be it a fresh start or revamp, we take every detail into account to carve your space in the competitive market. iDriven Global Tech designs your exclusive and unique logo, drafts your tagline, and defines your vision and mission. We also develop a USP exclusive for your customers.

Marketing Strategy:

Defining a perfect and flawless marketing strategy is one of the key processes in Branding. Our experts use all the resources like your brand, tagline, and unique message in every hard and soft copy of digital marketing collaterals. Establishing the reach of your logo helps people to recognize you in one look.

Brand Development:

Once the groundwork is laid strong, the next step is to establish the digital presence of your brand. Our team achieves this by updating your website regularly, creating new marketing campaigns, and establishing sufficient media resources. We take care of every requirement for launching and running promotions and events for your campaigns.

Marketing Campaign Launches:

Launching a marketing campaign is equally as important as marketing your brand. We take care of every detail from planning to launching and provide every support that might be needed from launch to analysis after the event.

Assess and Relaunch:

We track the events while launching them to analyze the return on investment (ROI). Our team stays in touch with you regularly to share the reports and discuss the metrics. The end results help in enhancing the plans for the ongoing marketing campaigns.

Account and Project Management:

Our professionals watch your account regularly ad generates new ideas that could surpass the existing initiatives. Ongoing management of the account helps us with identifying even the small issues and adjusting as we go instead of messing big in the long run.
