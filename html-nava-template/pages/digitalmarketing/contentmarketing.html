Content Marketing

Content plays a very major role in promoting a digital business. Content not just refers to the text on the web page. It includes all visible data such as images, videos, banners, and more. Creating the best online content is important, however, marketing this online content is even more important. iDriven Global Tech draws a perfect content marketing strategy, creates content on regular basis, and also promotes them online on different relevant channels.

Our approach:

Business Strategy Alignment

As the very first step, we sit with you in understanding your business profile from knowing your history and learning your product and services to knowing your competitors. Then we start drafting an exclusive content marketing strategy for your business based on your goals, previous strategy, target audience, brand value, gaps in your content, products, and services, and so on.

Content Strategy Development

After understanding your business in and outs, our strategists sit with you for developing a long-term, customized, and sustainable content marketing strategy. These strategies are mainly focused on the business objectives and target audience.

Extensive Research

Our content marketing strategists scrutinize and analyze every aspect to craft the perfect strategy after in-depth research. The aspects of research include keyword research, market research, SERP research, competitor analysis, and more.

Content Development

Once the strategy I drafted after extensive research, the next step is to create a content calendar. This calendar generally has the type of content, days for posting the respective content type, and the social media channels in which they will be published. The planned content can be a minimum count of 3 or the maximum count of 10.

Content Creation

Our experienced content creators create custom hand-crafted content for your business based on the content calendar. It can be a blog post, image post, online guide, or even a video.

Content Optimization

Once the content is created, we optimize it to fit the platform on which it is to be posted and also to drive maximum traffic and visit.

Content Promotion

After publishing the content, our link-building team takes care of promoting the content on various platforms through influence marketing and other content marketing techniques for better reach.

Results Measurement and Reporting

The main purpose of creating an effective content marketing strategy is to analyze how the content is performing and to make necessary changes as required. The final step is to track the results of every campaign and generate regular reports. These reports are regularly shared with you for understanding the improvements. Our team analyzes these reports and makes necessary tweaks for better enhancements and improvements.
