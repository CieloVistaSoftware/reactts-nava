Email Marketing Services

Keeping your customers updated on your products and services is more important. In that way, email marketing helps to communicate with your existing customers about any new offers, promotions, or product launches. It is also a powerful channel to get new customers through cold mail and newsletters. iDriven Global Tech provides top-notch email marketing services to send regular newsletters and emails to your existing customers and also surveys and forms for acquiring new customers.

Benefits of email Marketing:

  • Build Customer Loyalty – You can build your reputation among the customers by sending regular updates of your business
  • Reach customers in real-time – E-mails take the information straight to their inboxes in real-time
  • Connect with Different Audiences – You can connect with people from any location and any language.
  • Optimize your time and budget – You can save the money and time that you spend on meeting everyone individually.
  • Test Email Campaigns and Drive Strong Results – We can test the campaigns before publishing and analyze the result for strong results and maximum positive leads.
  • Track Your Analytics – You can track who has opened the mail, who has not, who has responded, and who has deleted.
  • Communicate in-depth information – You can end short and crisp info of 2 lines or draft a detailed information letter for 2 pages.
  • Gain better brand recognition – It helps with gaining better recognition among other brands
  • Increase website traffic – The number of visits through emails will increase.

Drip Marketing

Drip marketing utilizes automated email marketing software to send automated emails according to the timeframes and personalized based on the user actions. It is mostly used to send offers, teasers, launches, and brand updates in a short content template.

Three types of drip marketing:

Top-Of-Funnel (TOFU) Drip

Educational Drip

Re-engagement Drip
